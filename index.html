About Your Tutor

Baiba helps students build a practical foundation in Mandarin with clear explanations, real-life examples, and a calm learning rhythm. This website is the central place for basic tutor information and student tools.

Why Learn Chinese with Baiba

Lessons are practical, goal-oriented, and adapted to each student. Based on Baiba's teaching approach, students build confidence step by step.

  • Speak with Chinese speakers in real situations.
  • Practice writing and typing Chinese characters.
  • Think in Chinese and understand the language logic.
  • Read original texts and build natural comprehension.
  • Prepare for the HSK Chinese language exam.
  • Apply for study programs abroad with a stronger language foundation.
