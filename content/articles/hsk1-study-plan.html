Weeks 1 to 2: Build the foundation

Start with core vocabulary and sentence structures. Practice short listening clips and shadowing to improve pronunciation and rhythm.

This sequence reflects the way a native Chinese speaker tutor builds confidence first, then accuracy.

Weeks 3 to 5: Expand comprehension

Increase reading volume with simple dialogues and short paragraphs. Continue vocabulary review while adding regular dictation tasks.

  • Study new words in thematic sets.
  • Review old words daily with quick recall checks.
  • Mix listening and reading tasks in one session.

Weeks 6 to 8: Exam simulation and correction

Introduce timed mini-tests and full mock sessions. Track recurring mistakes and convert them into targeted revision lists.

By the final week, focus on confidence, consistency, and calm execution during timed practice.
